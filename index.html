About Spot CTR

This website was created with a simple purpose: to help traders save time and access important campaign information more efficiently. In many cases, users need to visit multiple announcements and pages to check different trading campaigns, which can be time-consuming and inconvenient. This platform brings the key campaign information together in one place so that users can quickly view active campaigns, check their remaining time, and understand the basic reward structure without searching through multiple sources. You may notice that this website currently lists only spot trading campaigns and does not include futures trading campaigns. This decision is intentional. In my personal view, futures trading carries a very high level of risk and many participants experience significant losses rather than consistent profits. Additionally, from an Islamic financial perspective, many scholars consider futures and other derivative-based trading structures to be problematic because they often involve excessive uncertainty, speculation, or elements that resemble gambling, which are discouraged in Islamic finance principles. Another important point: the estimated reward values shown here are based on personal analysis. The final distribution depends on total participation. Always conduct your own research before participating.
