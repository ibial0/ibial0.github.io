About Spot CTR

⚠️ Disclaimer: Spot CTR is an independent, personal tracking tool. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Binance. All reward estimates are based on personal analysis only.
🧭 What is Spot CTR?

Spot CTR is a personal tracking platform built to help Binance spot traders keep up with active trading campaigns — all in one clean interface. Instead of hunting through multiple pages, you can check estimated rewards, timers, and campaign details right here.

📊 How Are Estimates Calculated?

All reward estimates are based on personal analysis. They are calculated using available campaign parameters such as pool size, participant count, and trading volume requirements. These estimates are not guaranteed to be accurate. Always treat them as rough guidance.

🎯 Which Campaigns Are Listed?

Only campaigns offering a genuine earning opportunity to regular traders are listed. Top leaderboard positions (e.g. Rank #1–1,000 with fixed large prizes) are excluded from calculations since those are dominated by institutional-volume traders. Only the general pool is calculated.

⏳ Timeliness & Updates

Spot CTR is maintained by a single person. There may be a short delay before new campaigns appear. Campaign parameters may take a few days to update after launch. Your patience is appreciated.

📣 Share the Platform

If Spot CTR has helped you — please share it with fellow traders! Every share helps keep this project alive. 😁
