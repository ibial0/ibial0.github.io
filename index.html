About Spot CTR

⚠️ Disclaimer: Spot CTR is a personal, independent tracking tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Binance in any way. All reward estimates are based on personal analysis and are provided for informational purposes only. Never make financial decisions based solely on this data.
🧭 What is Spot CTR?

Spot CTR is a personal tracking platform built to help Binance spot traders keep up with active trading campaigns — all in one clean, organized interface. Instead of hunting through multiple pages on Binance, you can check estimated rewards, timers, and campaign details right here. The platform focuses exclusively on spot trading campaigns and is maintained by a single individual as a personal project.

📊 How Are Estimates Calculated?

All reward estimates shown on this platform are based on personal analysis and independent research. Figures are calculated using available campaign parameters such as pool size, participant count, and trading volume requirements. These estimates are not guaranteed to be accurate — actual rewards depend on real-time market conditions, total participation, and Binance's final distribution. Always treat these numbers as rough guidance, not a guarantee.

To account for real-world variability, displayed estimates may be slightly adjusted above or below the raw calculated value. This helps reduce the gap between estimated and actual outcomes, and is done entirely in good faith.

🎯 Which Campaigns Are Listed Here?

Only campaigns that offer a genuine earning opportunity to regular, everyday traders are added here. Campaigns are intentionally filtered as follows:

If a campaign offers a separate prize pool for top-ranked traders (e.g., Rank #1–100 or Rank #1–1,000 receiving a fixed large reward), that exclusive portion is excluded from calculations. For example: if a campaign awards $100,000 to the top 1,000 traders and splits $800,000 among everyone ranked 1,001 and beyond — only the general reward (1,001+) is calculated and displayed here.

This is intentional. Top leaderboard positions are almost always dominated by market makers and institutional-volume traders. The general reward pool is where most everyday traders can realistically participate and benefit.

⏳ A Note on Timeliness & Updates

Spot CTR is maintained by a single person. When Binance launches a new campaign or tournament, there may be a short delay — sometimes a few hours, sometimes a day or more — before it appears here. Campaign parameters (such as estimated participant count or volume data) may also take a few days to update after a campaign goes live.

Your patience is sincerely appreciated. Every update is made as promptly as possible.

🔍 Do Your Own Research

Always verify campaign details directly on Binance's official platform before committing any funds. This platform exists to save you time and give you a useful starting point — not to make decisions for you. Past estimates do not guarantee future accuracy. Trade responsibly.

📣 Share the Platform

If Spot CTR has saved you time, helped you spot a good campaign, or made your trading life a little easier — please share it with your fellow traders! Every share helps keep this project alive and growing. 😁
