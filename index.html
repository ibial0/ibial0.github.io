About Spot CTR

This website was created with a simple purpose: to help traders save time and access important campaign information more efficiently. In many cases, users need to visit multiple announcements and pages to check different trading campaigns, which can be time-consuming and inconvenient. This platform brings the key campaign information together in one place so that users can quickly view active campaigns, check their remaining time, and understand the basic reward structure without searching through multiple sources. By presenting everything in a single dashboard, users can plan their participation more effectively and make quicker decisions. You may notice that this website currently lists only spot trading campaigns and does not include futures trading campaigns. This decision is intentional. In my personal view, futures trading carries a very high level of risk and many participants experience significant losses rather than consistent profits. Because of these risks, I do not wish to promote or encourage this type of trading. Additionally, from an Islamic financial perspective, many scholars consider futures and other derivative-based trading structures to be problematic because they often involve excessive uncertainty, speculation, or elements that resemble gambling, which are discouraged in Islamic finance principles. For this reason, this platform focuses only on spot trading-related campaigns and avoids listing futures trading opportunities. Another important point concerns the estimated reward values shown on this website. The reward per one thousand dollars of trading volume displayed here is based on personal analysis and publicly available campaign information. However, the final distribution of rewards in many campaigns depends on the total participation and overall trading volume of all participants. Because of this, the actual reward received may vary and could be higher or lower than the estimate presented here. Users should always conduct their own research and review the official campaign announcements before participating in any trading activity. This website is intended purely as an informational dashboard designed to simplify campaign tracking and help users stay informed more efficiently.
