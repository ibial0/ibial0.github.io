About Spot CTR

This is not a business website. Always verify campaign details directly on Binance's official platform before making any decisions.

🧭 What is Spot CTR?

Spot CTR is a personal tracking platform built to help Binance spot traders keep up with active trading campaigns, all in one clean and organized interface. Instead of hunting through multiple Binance pages, you can check estimated rewards, countdowns, difficulty levels, and campaign details right here in one place.

This platform focuses exclusively on Binance spot trading campaigns. It was created out of a genuine need to simplify how traders track their participation in these campaigns and save valuable time in the process.

📊 How Are Estimates Calculated?

All reward estimates shown on this platform are based on personal analysis and independent research. Figures are calculated using available campaign parameters such as total pool size, number of participants, minimum trading volume requirements, and any publicly visible leaderboard data.

For volume-based campaigns, the estimate shows approximately how many tokens you might earn per $1,000 of trading volume. For equal-share campaigns, it shows the estimated share per participant based on the current participant count.

These estimates are not guaranteed to be accurate. Actual rewards depend on real-time market conditions, total participation at campaign end, and Binance's final distribution. Always treat these numbers as rough guidance and not a guarantee.

As an additional safety measure, displayed estimates may be slightly adjusted above or below the raw calculated value to reduce large discrepancies between shown estimates and actual distributions.

🎯 Which Campaigns Are Listed Here?

Only campaigns that offer a genuine, realistic earning opportunity to regular, everyday traders are added here. Many Binance campaigns include two separate reward pools: one for top-ranked traders (e.g. top 200 or top 1,000 with large fixed prizes), and a general pool shared among all other participants.

The top-rank special prizes are excluded from calculations here. Those positions are almost always dominated by market makers and institutional traders, not regular traders. For example:

A campaign awards $100,000 to the Top 1,000 ranked traders, and $800,000 shared equally among all traders ranked 1,001 and above. On Spot CTR, only the $800,000 general pool is calculated and displayed.

⏳ A Note on Timeliness and Updates

Spot CTR is maintained by a single person. When Binance launches a new campaign or tournament, it may take some time before it appears here. Campaign parameters may also take a few days to update after a campaign goes live.

Your patience is sincerely appreciated. If you don't see a campaign here yet, it will likely be added soon. Please check back later.

⚠️ Reward Delays and Postponements

Occasionally, Binance postpones a reward distribution deadline, sometimes indefinitely. When this happens, the affected campaign on Spot CTR will be marked with a ⚠️ Delayed badge, or a ⏳ TBD badge if no new date has been announced. Important notices about delays are shown directly on the homepage as a highlighted notice card.

🔍 Do Your Own Research

This platform exists to save you time and give you a useful starting point — not to make trading decisions for you. Always verify campaign details directly on Binance's official platform before committing any funds. No information here should be taken as financial advice. Trade responsibly.
